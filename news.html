PNC SportsFest 2025 Highlights

PNC Announces the New Mr. and Ms. PNC 2025

Students show confidence, talent, and school pride in this year’s pageant

Pamantasan ng Cabuyao proudly crowned the winners of Mr. and Ms. PNC 2025 in a night filled with cheers, excitement, and school spirit. Students from different colleges came together to support their representatives, making the event lively and memorable.

The main titles were awarded to Mr. Jerwin Avelino from the College of Engineering and Ms. Krizzy Maegan Algire from the College of Education. Both stood out with their confidence, clear answers, and inspiring messages for their fellow students. Their win reflects their hard work and dedication as proud PNC students.

The runners-up also showed strong performances. 1st Runners-Up were Mr. John Floyd Gayares (College of Education) and Ms. Reinnee Labarosa (CBAA). 2nd Runners-Up were Mr. Miguelsito Alvaro and Ms. Jasmine Marie Dailo from the College of Arts and Sciences, while 3rd Runners-Up were Mr. Don Carlos Miguel Abella (College of Computing Studies) and Ms. Brea Arbi Dayao (College of Engineering).

This year’s pageant highlighted not only beauty and talent but also the courage and passion of PNC students. The event reminded everyone that PNC is a community that supports and celebrates its students’ dreams, achievements, and growth.

CBAA Claims the Overall Championship for PNC Sportsfest 2025

Colleges show strong teamwork and school spirit in this year’s competition

The College of Business, Accountancy & Administration (CBAA) was proudly named the Overall Champion of the PNC Sportfest 2025 after earning an impressive 520 points. Their consistent performance in both sports and academic events helped them take the top spot and bring home the championship title.

Following closely behind is the College of Engineering, which secured the title of 2nd Overall Champion with 490 points. Their strong teamwork and effort in multiple events showed the college’s growing competitiveness and dedication.

Taking the 3rd Overall Champion spot is the College of Education, finishing with 365 points. Their athletes and performers displayed great determination and school spirit throughout the competition.

The remaining colleges also delivered commendable efforts: College of Computing Studies (320 points), College of Arts and Sciences (300 points), and College of Health and Allied Science (175 points).

PNC Recognizes Outstanding Coaches of SportsFest 2025

Educators honored for guiding student-athletes with skill, dedication, and heart

PNC proudly honored its Best Coaches of the Year during SportsFest 2025, celebrating the mentors who guided student-athletes with patience, discipline, and encouragement.

Track Event: Prof. Isabelita Isip | Basketball: Engr. Juancho Aragon | Badminton: Prof. Diorene Nica Serrano | Volleyball: Engr. John Wendell Arroyo | Chess: Engr. Julius Caezar Caparas.

These awards show how dedicated and passionate PNC coaches are—not only in teaching skills, but also in building character, sportsmanship, and unity among students.

CAS Back-to-Back Victory in PNC Cheerdance 2025

Colleges show school spirit and creativity in an exciting competition

The College of Arts and Sciences (CAS) once again claimed the top spot in this year’s PNC Cheerdance Competition, marking a back-to-back win for their high-energy and well-coordinated performance.

2nd Place: College of Business, Accountancy & Administration (CBAA) | 3rd Place: College of Computing Studies (CCS).

This year’s cheerdance competition highlighted not only skill and athleticism, but also the enthusiasm and unity of PNC students, making it a memorable celebration of student talent.
